# Kodiak Cast Product Blueprint

## One-liner

Kodiak Cast is the command center for planning, launching, and maintaining podcasts.

## Core problem

Most people do not fail at podcasting because they lack a microphone. They fail because they lack a repeatable system.

Common pain points:

- They do not know what their show is actually about.
- They do not have enough episode ideas.
- They do not know how to structure episodes.
- They do not know who to invite as guests.
- They record a few episodes and lose rhythm.
- They do not have a launch checklist.
- They confuse “starting a podcast” with buying gear.

## Product position

Kodiak Cast is not a recording tool first. It is the operating system around the podcast.

## Main modules

### Dashboard

Shows what to do next, current show status, upcoming episodes, launch progress, and consistency reminders.

### Podcast Blueprint

Defines the show name, audience, promise, tone, format, content pillars, cadence, and first-season direction.

### Episode Pipeline

Tracks episode ideas through stages:

- Idea
- Outline
- Scheduled
- Recorded
- Published

### Episode Builder

Turns a topic into:

- hook
- segment outline
- guest questions
- talking points
- closing CTA
- social clip ideas

### Guest CRM

Tracks potential guests, episode angles, contact status, outreach messages, and prep notes.

### Launch Checklist

Guides the creator through everything needed to launch cleanly.

### Maintenance System

Helps the creator keep publishing after the launch excitement fades.

## Killer feature

The “Build My Next Episode” button.

It should generate:

- best topic for this week
- title
- hook
- outline
- talking points
- optional guest suggestions
- clip ideas
- show notes draft
- social caption

## MVP boundary

Do not build recording, audio editing, hosting, analytics, sponsorship marketplaces, or payment systems in the first version.

Build the planning workflow first.
